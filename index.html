3D Art

Three-dimensional image

3D graphics - a section of computer graphics, a set of techniques and tools designed to depict three-dimensional objects. The three-dimensional image on the plane differs from the two-dimensional in that it includes the construction of a geometric projection of a three-dimensional model on the plane using specialized programs.

Where 3D is created

Programs

Daz3D
a powerful tool for creating animations and scenes by manipulating pre-installed models
This allows you to use models of people, vehicles, animals and objects. All you have to do is choose your models, choose the right accessories and start creating digital animation scenes.
Mixamo
3D animation software
A reliable tool for creating animated 3D characters. It is best that you do not need 3D knowledge to use this software. Mixamo offers a huge library of animations to choose from. You don't need to animate, for example, every step your character takes. Just go to the Mixamo store and get the animation you need for your needs.
Blender
free set of programs for creating open source 3D
It supports the entire 3D pipeline, including rigging, simulation, simulation, animation, tracking composition and movement, video editing and game creation. Blender is equipped with a built-in powerful direct tracking engine called Cycles. Blender offers a wide range of modeling tools that make it easy to transform, create and edit your models.
Cinema 4D Studio
animation software that you can use to easily create characters
